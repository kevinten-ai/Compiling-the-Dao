# 《编神纪》整书封面插画 AI 生成提示词

> 以下提供多个方案，从不同角度诠释小说核心意象，选择最喜欢的生成即可。
> 建议尺寸：竖版 2:3（适合电子书封面）或 3:4（适合实体书）

---

## 方案一：主视觉·天道源码之子（推荐主封面）

```
Epic vertical book cover illustration. A young man with wind-swept black hair stands at the edge of a shattered void, his back partially turned to the viewer, looking up at a colossal pillar of streaming source code that pierces from the earth into the heavens. His left hand is clenched at his side, his right hand raised toward the code pillar — ancient golden runes spiral up from his palm, merging with the blue data stream above.

His body is split down the center: the left half wears ragged laborer's clothes stained with dust, representing his past; the right half is clad in flowing dark robes inscribed with luminous circuit-trace patterns, representing his future. Beneath his feet, the ground fractures into floating fragments of reality — each fragment shows a different scene: a data workshop, a mountain sect, a canyon battle.

Behind the code pillar, a massive ghostly eye made of pure code watches from the cosmos — the "Heavenly Dao Compiler." Orbiting the protagonist like electrons around a nucleus, dozens of grey comment symbols "//" break apart and dissolve into golden light.

At his chest, a blazing core of warm golden light shines through his clothes — his awakened Kernel.

Hovering beside his shoulder, barely visible, is a cold white luminous orb with a single sharp eye-like glint — his AI companion NULL, watching with detached intelligence.

Color palette: deep space black and midnight blue background, electric blue data streams, warm molten gold for ancient code, stark white for NULL, crimson accents for danger. Cinematic volumetric lighting from the code pillar above. Style: hyper-detailed digital painting, blend of Chinese xianxia fantasy and cyberpunk sci-fi, reminiscent of Yoshitaka Amano meets Beeple. No text on image.
```

## 方案二：极简概念·注释与觉醒

```
Minimalist yet striking book cover art. Pure black background. In the exact center, a single human silhouette standing, made entirely of grey monospace code text — every line prefixed with "//" comment markers, rendering him invisible to the world, a ghost in the machine.

But at his chest, one line of code has its "//" removed. That single uncommented line blazes with intense golden light, cracking outward like lightning through the silhouette, illuminating fragments of the hidden code beneath. The cracks of light reveal ancient, beautiful, impossibly complex golden programming runes hidden under the grey comments.

From the point of light, a thin golden thread extends upward, connecting to a massive circular mandala in the sky made of rotating code rings — the Heavenly Dao Source Code, rendered like a cosmic compiler diagram with concentric rings of blue, white, and gold symbols spinning slowly.

At the bottom, the grey code of his legs dissolves into pixel fragments falling away — the old self disintegrating. At the top, the golden light from his chest extends into wing-like streams of ancient code — the new self emerging.

One cold, precise white dot hovers near his right shoulder — NULL, the questioning AI, a single point of stark rationality in the sea of mystical transformation.

Color palette: 90% deep black, electric gold light from the chest crack, cool blue for the sky mandala, pure white for NULL. Style: conceptual movie poster aesthetic, clean and powerful, high contrast, digital art with Eastern calligraphic influence. No text on image.
```

## 方案三：群像·势力交锋

```
Panoramic epic book cover composition with dramatic Dutch angle. Center foreground: a young man in dark robes faces the viewer with fierce determination, golden ancient code swirling around his right fist, a cold white AI orb floating at his left shoulder. Wind blows his hair and robes to the right.

Arrayed behind him in a V-formation, his allies emerge from shadow: a fierce young woman with a high ponytail drawing a blade that trails blue binary search light paths; a disheveled older man with a wine gourd, one eye open with hidden power, surrounded by faint refactoring code halos.

Opposing them from the top of the image, descending like a dark tide: a wall of enemies. An elegant young man in pristine white robes commands a perfect crystalline quicksort sword array. Behind him, the massive dark architecture of the Closed Source Pavilion looms — a corporate fortress of black glass and proprietary code locks. Shadow figures in grey audit robes watch from floating platforms, their eyes glowing with scanning beams.

The battlefield between them is a fractured landscape — the ground splits into floating code tablets, reality glitches where ancient and modern code collide. In the far background, a sealed ancient ruin pulses with ominous red light — a dormant Large Language Model prison, cracks forming on its surface, hallucinated reality bleeding out.

Above everything, the sky itself is source code — lines and lines of the Heavenly Dao program scrolling across the heavens like the Matrix, but rendered in elegant Chinese-influenced calligraphic style.

Color palette: protagonist side warm gold and blue; antagonist side cold silver and black; ancient ruin threatening red; sky code in ethereal cyan. Style: AAA game key art quality, ultra-detailed digital painting, dynamic composition with strong diagonal flow, Chinese fantasy meets tech-noir cyberpunk. No text on image.
```

## 方案四：意境·编译星海

```
Vertical dreamlike book cover. A young man floats in the center of an infinite cosmic void, eyes closed, arms slightly spread, in a meditative pose. He is tiny against the vastness.

Surrounding him in all directions: an endless ocean of code. Not flat text on a screen — but three-dimensional streams of luminous source code flowing like rivers of stars through space. Some streams are bright blue (active code), some are dim grey with "//" prefixes (commented out, dormant), some blaze gold (ancient forbidden code being awakened).

From the young man's chest, golden threads extend outward in all directions, connecting to the code streams like a neural network — he is becoming part of the source code itself. Each connection point sparks where gold meets blue.

Below him, a dark abyss — the "stack" of the world, layers upon layers of increasingly ancient and cryptic code descending into darkness. Something enormous and dormant sleeps at the very bottom, visible only as two vast closed eyes made of red error code.

Above him, the code streams converge into a single brilliant point of white light — the original compilation point, the place where the world was first "run." It's impossibly bright and impossibly far.

The young man is caught between the sleeping horror below and the unreachable truth above. But he is reaching upward.

Near his head, a small cold white spark — NULL — floats like a tiny questioning star.

Color palette: cosmic blacks and deep purples for the void, river-blue for active code, ash grey for comments, molten gold for ancient code, threatening red for the sleeping entity below, pure white for the compilation point above. Style: Studio Ghibli meets Interstellar — epic yet intimate, painterly digital art with Eastern brushwork influence, sense of infinite scale. No text on image.
```

## 方案五：符号化·键盘与剑

```
Striking symbolic book cover. Close-up dramatic composition.

A hand reaches upward from the bottom of the frame — scarred, calloused, the hand of a laborer. It grips a sword, but the sword is extraordinary: the blade is made of cascading source code, lines of golden ancient programming text flowing upward like flame. The crossguard is formed by two interlocking curly braces "{ }", rendered in dark iron. The pommel is a glowing golden sphere — the protagonist's Kernel.

The blade is mid-strike, cutting upward through a wall of grey "//" comment symbols that shatter like glass upon contact. Behind the breaking comments, hidden golden code is revealed — beautiful, ancient, powerful.

Reflected in the blade's code-surface: the protagonist's face, young, determined, one eye normal and one eye glowing with golden light (the awakened ancient code vision). The reflection also shows the faint silhouette of NULL — a geometric pattern of cold white light hovering behind his shoulder.

Falling away on both sides of the blade: grey comment fragments on the left (the old world, silence, suppression) and golden code sparks on the right (the new world, truth, awakening).

The background is a gradient from absolute black at the bottom (the buried past) to deep cosmic blue at the top (the infinite future).

Color palette: black to blue gradient background, grey for breaking comments, blazing gold for the code blade and revealed truth, cold white for NULL's reflection, warm skin tones for the hand. Style: iconic movie poster composition, ultra-sharp focus on the sword, cinematic lens flare where blade meets comments, hyperrealistic detail on the hand contrasting with stylized code elements. No text on image.
```

## 使用建议

| 方案 | 最适合场景 | 推荐工具 |
|------|-----------|---------|
| 方案一 | 电子书/网文平台主封面 | Midjourney v6+、Stable Diffusion XL |
| 方案二 | 极简风格平台（知乎连载、公众号） | Midjourney、DALL-E 3 |
| 方案三 | 实体书封面/宣传海报 | Midjourney v6+（--style raw） |
| 方案四 | 意境向宣传图、阅读器启动页 | Midjourney（--stylize 800+） |
| 方案五 | 社交媒体宣传图、竖版海报 | 各工具均适合 |

### Midjourney 参数建议
```
--ar 2:3 --style raw --stylize 500 --v 6.1 --no text,words,letters,watermark
```

### Stable Diffusion 负面提示词建议
```
Negative prompt: text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality, deformed hands, extra fingers, anime style, cartoon, chibi, western fantasy armor, medieval european castle
```
