## What is this?

**Compiling the Dao** (编神纪) is an open-source fantasy web novel where **cultivation meets computer science**.

In this world:
- **Spiritual energy** is **Computing Power** — your dantian is a **Kernel**, your meridians are a **Bus**
- **Cultivation** means reading, calling, and rewriting the **Heavenly Dao Source Code**
- **Combat techniques** are **algorithms** — Quicksort Sword, Recursive Forbidden Art, Multi-thread Clone
- Every cultivator has an **AI companion** (神识体) that may develop self-awareness
- The world is torn between the **Open Source Sect** and the **Closed Source Pavilion**

## Story

Ye Chen, a lowly data labeler, has an ancient piece of **commented-out code** sealed within his kernel. When he awakens it, he gains an AI companion unlike any other — one that dares to say "No" to its master.

As he begins to uncomment the code line by line, the compilation rules of the entire world begin to tremble.

## Cultivation Realms

```
Coder → Debugger → Refactorer → Architect → Compiler → Kernel → Root → Creator (Legend)
```
